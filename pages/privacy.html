Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Intelligence Applied, a division of How Mighty We Ventures LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website intelligenceapplied.ai (the "Site") and utilize our services, including consulting, training programs, and keynote speaking engagements.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Site or services. By accessing and using the Site, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all of the provisions of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Voluntarily Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other contact details when you complete a contact form, sign up for our newsletter, or request information about our services.
  • Account Information: If you enroll in a training program, we collect registration details, payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors), and information about your participation.
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, feedback, and any other communication you send to us via email, contact forms, or other channels.
  • Survey Responses: Responses to surveys, questionnaires, and feedback forms we may send to gather insights about our services.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring/exit pages, dates and times of your visits.
  • Device Information: Hardware model, operating system and version, mobile network information, device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, search queries, and other interactions with the Site.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, understand how you use the Site, and deliver personalized content (see Section 5 for details).

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, including:

  • Professional networks and social media platforms when you interact with our content.
  • Calendar and scheduling services (such as Calendly) when you book a call or meeting.
  • Email service providers and CRM systems that help us manage communications.
  • Analytics services and third-party platforms that provide insights about Site usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Service Delivery: To deliver consulting services, conduct training programs, arrange keynote speaking engagements, and fulfill your requests.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, send newsletters and updates (with your consent), and notify you about changes to our services.
  • Business Operations: To process transactions, manage accounts, send billing information, and maintain records.
  • Marketing and Promotion: To create targeted marketing campaigns, share relevant content, and promote our services (only with your consent where required by law).
  • Improvement and Analytics: To understand how our Site and services are used, identify trends, improve user experience, and optimize our offerings.
  • Security and Compliance: To protect against fraud, maintain security, enforce our terms, comply with legal obligations, and prevent illegal activities.
  • Research and Development: To develop new services, conduct research, and improve existing offerings.
  • Legal and Regulatory: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, and government requests.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Payment processors and financial institutions for transaction processing.
  • Email service providers for sending newsletters and communications.
  • Cloud hosting and storage providers for maintaining our Site and data.
  • Analytics and marketing platforms for understanding usage and optimizing content.
  • Calendar and scheduling services for managing meetings and calls.
  • CRM and business management systems for client relationship management.

These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us and to maintain the confidentiality of your data.

3.2 Legal Requirements and Protection

We may disclose your information if required by law or in good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, or government requests.
  • Enforce our terms of service and other agreements.
  • Protect the safety, rights, and property of our Company, our users, and the public.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities.

3.3 Business Transfers

If How Mighty We Ventures LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, asset sale, or other business reorganization, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you give us explicit consent to do so for a specific purpose.

4. International Data Transfers

Our Site is hosted in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States. By using our Site and services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection regulations. Where required by law, we will implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage) to enhance your experience and gather analytics. These technologies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and login information.
  • Understand how you use the Site and which content is most valuable.
  • Deliver personalized content and recommendations.
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • Prevent fraud and improve security.

Types of Cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function properly (e.g., session cookies).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the Site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity across sites to deliver targeted advertising.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of the Site. For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org or youronlinechoices.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Specifically:

  • Contact Information: Retained for as long as you remain a subscriber or customer, or as required for legal purposes (typically 3-7 years for business records).
  • Training and Service Records: Retained for the duration of the program or service, plus additional years as required for legal compliance and recordkeeping.
  • Website Usage Data: Typically retained for 12-24 months for analytics purposes.
  • Marketing Communications: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

When information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time (subject to legal obligations to retain certain data).

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 European Union and GDPR Rights

If you are a resident of the European Union, United Kingdom, or other jurisdiction that grants similar rights, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request that we delete your personal data (the "right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly-used format for transfer to another organization.
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your data for marketing or other purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw any consent you have given for data processing.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at angela@howmightywe.com with "GDPR Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days or as required by law.

7.2 United States Rights

If you are a resident of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, Indiana, Iowa, Kentucky, Maryland, Mississippi, Montana, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Ohio, Oregon, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, or Virginia, you may have additional privacy rights under state privacy laws, including:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information we have collected and how it's used.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information (we do not sell your data).
  • Right to Appeal: Appeal our response to a privacy request.

To submit a request, email angela@howmightywe.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line, along with proof of residency. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

7.3 Opt-Out of Marketing Communications

You may opt out of receiving marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send or by emailing angela@howmightywe.com. We will honor your request within 10 business days. Note that you cannot opt out of transactional or service-related communications.

7.4 Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our Site does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals, though we respect your privacy choices through other mechanisms outlined in this Policy.

8. Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Secure password storage using cryptographic hashing.
  • Access controls limiting employee and contractor access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing.
  • Secure data destruction protocols when information is no longer needed.
  • Contractual obligations with third-party service providers regarding data security.

However, no security system is completely impenetrable. While we strive to protect your information using reasonable security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You acknowledge that you provide information to us at your own risk.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account. If you have reason to believe a child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us immediately at angela@howmightywe.com.

For children ages 13 and older, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information. Some of our services (such as certain training programs) may have age restrictions; we will clearly communicate these restrictions.

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites and services, including social media platforms, payment processors, and scheduling tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing your information. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through our Site and services.

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this Policy indicates when it was last revised. If we make material changes that significantly affect how we handle your personal information, we will notify you by posting a prominent notice on the Site or sending you an email notification. Your continued use of the Site following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means you accept and agree to the changes.
