Frequently Asked Questions

Angela specializes in healthcare, pharmaceutical, medical research, and biotech industries, leveraging her 20+ years of experience in these sectors. However, her expertise in data governance, AI implementation, and digital transformation applies across industries. She works with organizations in financial services, technology, consumer goods, and other sectors looking to implement ethical, human-centered AI solutions.

Our standard implementation timeline is 10 weeks from start to deployment, broken down as: Discovery (Weeks 1-2), Strategy (Weeks 2-3), Development (Weeks 4-8), and Deployment (Weeks 9-10). However, the timeline can vary based on your organization's complexity, existing systems, and scope of the AI solution. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

While technical expertise is helpful, it's not required. Angela's approach includes comprehensive training and support for your team. We'll work with both technical and non-technical stakeholders to ensure everyone understands how to leverage the AI solution. Part of our commitment is empowering your team so you can maintain and optimize the system independently after implementation.

Data security and privacy are paramount, especially in regulated industries like healthcare. Angela brings extensive experience with HIPAA, GDPR, and other compliance requirements. Our AI implementations include robust data governance frameworks, encryption, access controls, and audit trails. We work closely with your security and compliance teams to ensure all solutions meet or exceed your organization's security standards and regulatory requirements.

Implementation is just the beginning. We provide ongoing optimization and support to ensure your AI solution continues to deliver value as your business evolves and as AI technology advances. This includes performance monitoring, regular optimization cycles, user support, and strategic recommendations for leveraging new AI capabilities. The specific support structure is customized based on your needs.

We establish clear metrics and KPIs during the strategy phase that directly align with your business objectives. These typically include measures like cost reduction, efficiency gains, revenue impact, time savings, quality improvements, or risk reduction—depending on your specific goals. We provide regular reporting on these metrics and help you track progress toward our 2X ROI guarantee. Transparency and measurable results are central to our approach.
