Keynotes & Speaking

Speaking Topics

Angela brings expertise, passion, and compelling storytelling to every keynote

Data, AI & Health Literacy for Kids

Teaching the next generation to be positive digital citizens. Angela shares her approach to making complex AI and data concepts accessible to elementary and middle school learners, empowering them to understand and shape the digital world.

AI for Good

How AI can address societal challenges when deployed ethically. This keynote explores the intersection of technology and social responsibility, showing how organizations can harness AI's power while ensuring equity and inclusivity.

The Role of AI in Medicine

Transforming clinical research and patient outcomes. Drawing from her 20+ years in healthcare and medical research, Angela discusses how AI is revolutionizing drug discovery, patient care, and clinical trial management.

Data Ownership & Ethics

Who owns our data and why it matters. A critical examination of data governance, privacy rights, and ethical frameworks. Angela helps audiences understand the implications of data ownership in healthcare, business, and society.

Overcoming Adversity

Personal stories of resilience that inspire action. Angela shares her journey of overcoming poverty, abuse, loss, and neurodiversity. These powerful narratives inspire audiences to overcome their own challenges and create positive change.

Human-Centered AI Implementation

Strategic AI adoption for business audiences. A practical guide to implementing AI in organizations while keeping humans at the center. Angela discusses change management, team preparation, and building an AI-ready culture.

About the Speaker

Angela Radcliffe is a dynamic speaker, best-selling author, and impact influencer with 20+ years of experience driving progress in medical research, healthcare marketing, and healthcare data and artificial intelligence.

As the author of "Quantum Kids Guardians of AI," she's dedicated to teaching children and organizations the skills needed to thrive in a digitally connected world. Her speaking style combines technical expertise with compelling personal stories of resilience and transformation.

Previously Head of Digital Performance Improvement and Innovation for Research and Early Development IT, Head of Enterprise Digital Strategy Innovation, and Head of Enterprise Data Governance at Bristol Myers Squibb, Angela has orchestrated transitions from molecular concepts to market-ready solutions and from boardroom strategies to transformative implementations.

Her keynotes inspire audiences to think differently about AI, data, ethics, and human potential—leaving lasting impressions that drive real change.
